## ✅ Questions You Should Ask Before Booking

### 1. Does the venue accommodate both adults and children?

A family-friendly venue allows everyone to enjoy the celebration together.

### 2. Are picnic and braai facilities available?

Outdoor dining creates a more relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere.

### 3. Is there entertainment included?

Swimming pools, sports fields, trampolines, and play areas keep guests entertained throughout the day.

### 4. Can larger groups be accommodated?

Whether you're hosting a church event, workplace celebration, or community gathering, flexibility is important.

### 5. Is overnight accommodation available?

Accommodation allows guests travelling from further away to turn the celebration into a relaxing weekend getaway.
